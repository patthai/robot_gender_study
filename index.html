Robot design preliminary study

Design is a crucial part of every technology. Great designs allow human to interact with technology in a friendly manner, thus results in high productivity by bringing out the full potential of the technology. The goal of this study is to understand the relationship between design of the robot and human perception toward them.

This study is a preliminary study which take approximately 3 minutes to complete.
